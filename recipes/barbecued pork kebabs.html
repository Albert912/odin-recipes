Barbecued Pork Kebabs

his Filipino pork kebab recipe is great for outdoor parties, especially picnics.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (4 pound) boneless pork loin, cut into 1 1/2-inch cubes
  • 10 bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Whisk the sugar, soy sauce, onion, garlic and black pepper together in a large bowl. Add the pork and toss to coat. Cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours. Overnight is best if possible.

  2. Step 2

    3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.

  3. Step 3

    4. Thread the pork onto the soaked skewers. Cook on the preheated grill until the pork is no longer pink in the center, 3 to 5 minutes per side.
